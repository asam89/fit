How It Works

Get started in 3 simple steps

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with email or Google. Set your timezone, units, and fitness goals in Settings.

2

Connect Telegram

Link your Telegram bot in Settings. Text, voice, or send photos of your meals — the AI logs everything.

3

Track & Improve

View your dashboard for daily macros, trends, training plans, and personalized coaching insights.

What You Can Do

Photo Meal Logging

Snap a photo of your meal — AI identifies foods and estimates calories, protein, carbs, fat, and more.

Voice & Text Tracking

Send a voice note or type naturally: "I had a chicken salad for lunch." The AI understands and logs it.

Dashboard & Macros

See daily calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, sugar, sodium, and water — with progress bars and targets.

Wearable Sync

Import health data from Apple Health, Samsung Health, Garmin, Fitbit, or Oura using the /sync command.

Training Plans

AI-generated weekly workout plans tailored to your goals. Check off activities from Telegram or dashboard.

Friends & Social

Invite friends, share progress, send nudges, and stay accountable together.
